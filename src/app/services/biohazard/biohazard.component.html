BIOHAZARD

If you have had a sewage issue, crime scene, infectious illness, or any other form of biohazards occur in your home or business, make sure to contact us. At MJ Builders, we are available 24/7 for your biohazard removal needs. We make sure to assess the damages and create a safe plan for removal. Following industry standards, we remove and dispose of the biohazard materials and items, as well as the affected parts of the home such as carpet or bedding which may have come in contact. Following the removal of the identified waste, we make sure your property is deodorized to its prior state.
